# Review #1: WordReference
~Many people have translators.~ Correction — most people have translators. It's on your phone! Google Translate, MS Translate... there are so many!

Many people think that Google Translate, of all translators, is best. I'm not going to lie, it's okay. But it direct-translates.
Many things in G Translate translate completely wrong — for proof, go to youtube and type "google translate sings pokemon theme". 

G Translate is the WORST online translator ever.

so what do we use?

~Microsoft Translator?~ No...  
~Siri or Cortana or whatever?~ Most definitley not...

Then what the heck do we use?

Welcome to WordReference. WordReference is the best translator ever. (they're not even sponoring me!) All you have to do is type
in a word, and all the translations — along with definitions, genders, and more — all appear. And best of all, it's free!

It has over 14 languages. 14! And it is extremely easy to use. It even has two words of the day with a mailing list!

Overall, [WordReference](https://wordreference.com) is a great translator. You should really use it.
